About

HANSONG INDUSTRY

Founded in 1993 and based in South Korea, HANSONG INDUSTRY is a specialized B2B manufacturer of high-quality chemical preparations for textile finishing, leather finishing, and flooring materials. With over 30 years of extensive experience and technical expertise, we deliver trusted products and services to partners worldwide. Through continuous research and development, we contribute to advancing the industry with innovative finishing solutions.
